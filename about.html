Our story

About us

Friends of Fieldway is a community-led initiative creating a safe, welcoming space where local residents of all ages can learn, connect, grow, and belong.

Our story

It started with a simple idea

Friends of Fieldway began as a conversation between local residents who shared a common concern: too many people in the community lacked a safe, consistent place to access support, connection, and opportunity.

Children needed somewhere to study after school. Young people needed positive activities and mentoring. Women and men needed spaces for support and social connection. Older residents needed a way to stay included in everyday community life.

What emerged was a vision for a multi-purpose community hub that could serve every generation, a building that would stay active from daytime through evening, with programmes designed around the real needs of local people. Friends of Fieldway was founded to make that vision a reality.

Our vision

A safe, vibrant, and supportive community space where people learn, connect, grow, and belong

Our mission is to create an inclusive and enriching community hub where local residents of all ages can access educational support, social connection, wellbeing activities, creative opportunities, and meaningful community engagement.

Our founder

The person behind the vision

Roy Simpson

Founder, Friends of Fieldway

Roy Simpson founded Friends of Fieldway with a clear purpose: to create a community hub where local residents of all ages can access the support, connection, and opportunity they deserve. Drawing on a deep understanding of the community's needs, Roy has led the development of the initiative's vision, programmes, and partnerships from the very beginning.
