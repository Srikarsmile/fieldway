About us

Friends of Fieldway is a community-led charity creating a safe, welcoming space where local residents of all ages can learn, connect, grow, and belong.

Our story

It started with a simple idea

Friends of Fieldway began as a conversation between local residents who shared a common concern: too many people in the community lacked a safe, consistent place to access support, connection, and opportunity.

Children needed somewhere to study after school. Young people needed positive activities and mentoring. Women and men needed spaces for support and social connection. Older residents needed a way to stay included in everyday community life.

What emerged was a vision for a multi-purpose community hub that could serve every generation, a building that would stay active from daytime through evening, with programmes designed around the real needs of local people. Friends of Fieldway was founded to make that vision a reality.

Our vision

A safe, vibrant, and supportive community space where people learn, connect, grow, and belong

Our mission is to create an inclusive and enriching community hub where local residents of all ages can access educational support, social connection, wellbeing activities, creative opportunities, and meaningful community engagement.

Our commitment

Inclusion is at the heart of everything we do

We believe that every person in our community deserves to feel welcome, valued, and supported regardless of their age, background, gender, ethnicity, or circumstance.

Our programmes are designed to be accessible and responsive to the needs of the community. We actively listen to local residents, adapt what we offer, and work hard to remove barriers to participation.

Whether someone is a parent looking for help with their child's education, a young person searching for guidance, an adult seeking connection, or an older resident wanting to stay involved, Friends of Fieldway is here for them.

We are committed to creating an environment where diversity is celebrated, where people feel safe to be themselves, and where the whole community can come together around shared goals of learning, wellbeing, and belonging.

Our values

What guides us every day

Caring

Everything we do is rooted in genuine care for the people in our community and their wellbeing.

Inclusive

We welcome everyone regardless of age, background, or circumstance. Our space belongs to all.

Reliable

Our community can count on us to be consistent, trustworthy, and always there when needed.

Hopeful

We believe in the potential of every person and the power of community to create positive change.
