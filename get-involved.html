Get involved

Friends of Fieldway thrives because of the people who support it. Whether you can give your time, share your skills, or partner with us, there is a meaningful way for you to help.

Volunteer with us

We are always looking for passionate people to join our team. Whether you can help with tutoring, mentoring, facilitating workshops, or supporting events, your time makes a real difference. No special qualifications needed, just a willingness to care.

Partner with us

Schools, community organisations, local businesses, and councils can partner with Friends of Fieldway to shape and strengthen our programmes. Together we can achieve more for the people who need it most.

Support our community work

Your support helps sustain the programmes that make a difference in people's lives. Whether it is funding, donations, or raising awareness, every contribution strengthens the community hub we are building together.

More ways to help

Every contribution matters

You do not need to make a big commitment to make a big difference. Here are some other ways you can support Friends of Fieldway.

Join an activity

Attend one of our programmes or events. Your presence helps build a stronger, more connected community.

View events

Spread the word

Tell friends, family, and neighbours about Friends of Fieldway. Word of mouth is one of the most powerful ways to grow our reach.

Refer someone

If you know someone who could benefit from our programmes, let them know we are here. We welcome referrals from individuals and organisations.
