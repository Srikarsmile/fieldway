Get involved

Get involved

Friends of Fieldway thrives because of the people who support it. Whether you can give your time, share your skills, or partner with us, there is a meaningful way for you to help.

Ways to help

Choose how you want to get involved

Start with the route that fits your time, skills, or organisation. We can help shape the details together.

Volunteer with us

We are always looking for passionate people to join our team. Whether you can help with tutoring, mentoring, facilitating workshops, or supporting events, your time makes a real difference. No special qualifications needed, just a willingness to care.

Partner with us

Schools, community organisations, local businesses, and councils can partner with Friends of Fieldway to shape and strengthen our programmes. Together we can achieve more for the people who need it most.

Support our community work

Your support helps sustain the programmes that make a difference in people's lives. Whether it is funding, donations, or raising awareness, every contribution strengthens the community hub we are building together.

Our commitment

Maximising social return on investment

We are committed to managing our community space responsibly and effectively to maximise its positive impact on the people we serve.

Better academic outcomes

Structured educational support helps students achieve stronger results and build lasting study habits.

Reduced social isolation

Regular programmes and gatherings create meaningful connections for adults and elderly residents alike.

Positive youth engagement

Safe and constructive activities provide young people with alternatives to negative influences.

Creativity and cultural expression

Arts, reading, and poetry programmes encourage personal expression and celebrate community culture.
